# PROTOTYPE_REVIEW_SCORECARD.md

## Purpose
Use one consistent rubric to compare the three prototype directions.

## Scoring
1 = weak
2 = below bar
3 = acceptable
4 = strong
5 = standout

## Evaluation dimensions
### 1. Annual planning clarity
Can a team understand the year plan quickly without explanation-heavy guidance?

### 2. Team planning utility
Does the prototype support active planning work, not just passive viewing?

### 3. Export readiness
Would a PNG/PDF from this direction already look review-ready?

### 4. Idea capture quality
Does the product support fuzzy-first planning well enough for real marketing use?

### 5. Resistance to PM-tool creep
Does it stay planning-first rather than feeling like project management software?

### 6. First-use comprehension
Would a marketing team understand how to use it on first exposure?

## Forced-choice questions
1. Which direction best protects the product from becoming another PM tool?
2. Which direction looks strongest in a crowded annual dataset?
3. Which direction would Andrew most likely want to review first?
4. Which direction creates the best hybrid base for v1 convergence?
