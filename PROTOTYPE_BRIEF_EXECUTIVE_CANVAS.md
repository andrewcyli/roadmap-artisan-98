# PROTOTYPE_BRIEF_EXECUTIVE_CANVAS.md

## Direction
Executive Canvas

## Objective
Produce the cleanest annual planning view for marketing teams and stakeholder review.

## Design thesis
If the product feels naturally deck-ready during normal use, it wins a strong differentiation point versus PM tools and generic calendars.

## Primary moment to optimize
A marketing lead opens the annual plan in front of teammates or leadership and can explain the full year without cleaning up the screen first.

## Core emphasis
- strongest annual scanability
- quiet UI chrome
- subtle but legible campaign grouping
- export quality nearly identical to live review mode

## Required prototype screens
1. project setup
2. default year view
3. quarter zoom state
4. campaign group expanded state
5. overlap stack state
6. export/review mode

## Interaction priorities
- quick add remains available but visually secondary
- campaign groups must read at a glance
- side panel should not dominate the layout
- filters stay lightweight and collapsible

## Visual rules
- high whitespace discipline
- restrained metadata on cards
- max 1-2 visible metadata signals per card
- muted grid and lane dividers
- group color should organize, not decorate

## Success criteria
- a crowded annual plan still looks calm
- export requires no slide cleanup
- team can scan major campaign arcs in under 30 seconds

## Failure signs
- cards feel too empty to support planning
- users must open panels constantly to understand context
- year view looks elegant only when dataset is sparse
